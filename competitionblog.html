Competition

Posted by Admin on 01/01/2018 in Web Dev

The existence of this world is based on Competition. Competition simply means a contest between each other to prove which one is the best. This creates unnecessary rivalry. In life in order to survive we have to face this competition. I would like to say the famous dialogue of the movie 3 idiots, “Life is a race, and if you don’t run fast you will be crushed”. There are innumerable types of competitions. Competitions related to sports, food, jobs, studies and many many more. The competition of sports is my personal favorite. I love sports; it’s a positive way for looking at competition. A conflict fought with peace and most of all its truly fun… Nature has only one rule and that is survival of the fittest. That’s why carnivores have to keep themselves in a good shape in order to acquire food. There are not many jobs and so many people that it creates even more Competition. In order to get job we have to study. Here comes the worst part. Competition in studies really does influence the mind in a bad way… Competition has its pros and cons. The good thing about competition is that it results in the development of the country. To prove their supremacy to others, they try to be better, this results in indirect development. The bad thing is that competition leads to rivalry among each other. Rivalry leads to conflicts and unnecessary disputes that in turn lead to enemies. And Enemies lead to WARS!!! In my opinion competition is a very good way to enhance one’s abilities to the fullest. But is there a need to compete with others? Is there a need to fight with others? What will you prove even if you win? If you really want to do competitions, do it with no other than yourself!!

Manage Your Priority

Posted by Manav Bahl on 19/03/2019 in Blogger

What is your priority? First of all “PRIORITY” means a matter of great importance or a primary issue My friend Larry has a couple of jobs to do. He has work from office, House cleaning and taking his dog for a walk. The question arises that which job must be done first! This is when it is very important to choose a job which has the most important need to be done.
How to choose a priority? Many jobs piling up can result into stress so one must try segregating jobs on the basis of Importance.
One must follow steps given:-
1. Making your TO-DO List
By making a To-Do list one can know the jobs to be done which will make the jobs easily visible for the achiever.
2. Write down everything you need to do
This will help to understand the work which needs to be done first and can also reduce stress.
3.Make the list visible
This is very important because it is possible for a person to forget the things to be done. One can pin it on the fridge or write it on a white board.
4. Rank your task and a tick mark after done.
Ranking and ticking sounds very lame but it helps you as a motivator as the task which was pending is done and you can see that.
5. Attacking the list!
Go for doing the task and trash it off. This will surely give you satisfaction!
Why does the priority change?
As we grow… our priority keeps on changing because we tend to choose jobs which have great importance. On doing this one must not forget that every job must be done and choosing a priority is just arranging the jobs as per importance.
As it is not bad to be Stress Free!
